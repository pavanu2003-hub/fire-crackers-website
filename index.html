Your Safety Matters

Stay Safe, Celebrate Smart

Follow these essential guidelines for a joyful and incident-free celebration

01

Keep Water Nearby

Always have a bucket of water and a sand bucket within reach before lighting any cracker.

02

Maintain Safe Distance

Keep at least 5 metres away after lighting. Use long matchsticks or agarbatti to ignite safely.

03

Never Re-ignite a Dud

If a cracker fails to fire, soak it in water immediately and dispose safely. Never retry.

04

Adult Supervision Required

Children must never handle crackers unsupervised. Assign a responsible adult for every session.

05

Wear Cotton Clothing

Dress in natural cotton during celebrations. Synthetic fabrics ignite quickly and can cause severe burns.

06

Store in Cool, Dry Place

Keep unused crackers in original packaging away from heat, moisture, and direct sunlight at all times.
